About Me

Mission-driven full stack developer with a passion for thoughtful UI design, collaboration. Focused on crafting clean & user‑friendly experiences. I am passionate about building excellent software that improves the lives of those around me.

I also have over 8 years of Professional Experience as a QA Tester / Analyst in Information Technology with extensive experience in performing Manual and Automated Testing on Web based, Desktop applications, Client-server applications and Mobile based applications. Extensive experience in Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) and in all phases of Quality Assurance Life Cycle.

Currently I'm working for Ultimate Software, Weston, Florida as Software Automation Specialist.

I’ve always sought out opportunities and challenges that are meaningful to me. Although my professional path has taken many twists and turns — from QA manual tester to QA automation Engineer, to small business owner and entrepreneur and now as a full stack web developer— I've never stopped engaging my passion to help others and solve problems.

As a web developer, I enjoy using my obsessive attention to detail, my unequivocal love for making things, and my mission-driven work ethic to literally change the world.

I specialize in creating software for clients ranging from individuals and small-businesses all the way to large enterprise corporations.
